<p><strong>The Multi-axis Piezoelectric Accelerator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Linear Accelerometer</li><li>Pendulous Accelerometer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Multi-axis Piezoelectric Accelerator market comprises two main types: Linear Accelerometers and Pendulous Accelerometers. Linear Accelerometers measure acceleration along a single axis, providing precise data for motion detection and control in various applications, such as automotive and aerospace. Pendulous Accelerometers, on the other hand, use a suspended mass to detect acceleration changes and are particularly suited for applications requiring higher sensitivity and versatility. Both types leverage piezoelectric materials to convert mechanical motion into electrical signals, enabling accurate measurement and analysis.</p></p>
