<p><p>The Electronic Anti-Reflection Glass market is primarily segmented into two types: Chemical Treatment and Physical Processing. Chemical Treatment involves applying anti-reflective coatings through chemical processes to enhance light transmission and minimize reflections. This method is often used in high-precision applications where optical clarity is crucial. Physical Processing, on the other hand, encompasses techniques like etching and polishing, which modify the glass surface to reduce glare. Both methods aim to improve the efficiency and visual performance of electronic displays and devices.</p></p>
