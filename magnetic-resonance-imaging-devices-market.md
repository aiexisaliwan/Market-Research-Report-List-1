<p><p>The Magnetic Resonance Imaging (MRI) devices market features several types: Traditional Closed MRI, Open MRI, and Standing or Sitting MRI. Traditional Closed MRI offers high-resolution images in a confined tube, ideal for detailed diagnostics but can cause discomfort for some patients. Open MRI provides a less claustrophobic experience with a wider opening, though it may have lower resolution. Standing or Sitting MRI allows patients to remain upright, accommodating specific conditions and enhancing comfort, catering to those who struggle with traditional positions.</p></p>
