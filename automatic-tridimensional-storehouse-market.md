<p><strong>The Automatic Tridimensional Storehouse Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Unit Load</li><li>Mini Load</li><li>Vertical Lift Module (VLM)</li><li>Carousel</li><li>Mid Load</li><li>Autostore</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automatic tridimensional storehouses enhance warehouse efficiency by utilizing vertical space and automating storage solutions. Unit load systems handle large items on pallets, while mini load systems specialize in small items, using cranes for retrieval. Vertical lift modules (VLM) maximize vertical space with trays that transport items between levels. Carousels rotate inventory for quick access, and mid-load systems bridge the gap between unit and mini loads. Autostore employs bins on an automated grid for efficient, dense storage and retrieval.</p></p>
