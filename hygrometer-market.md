<p><strong>The Hygrometer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Capacitive Hygrometer</li><li>Resistive Hygrometer</li><li>Thermal Hygrometer</li><li>Gravimetric Hygrometer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The hygrometer market consists of various types, including capacitive, resistive, thermal, and gravimetric hygrometers. Capacitive hygrometers measure humidity through changes in capacitance caused by moisture levels. Resistive hygrometers detect humidity by monitoring variations in electrical resistance. Thermal hygrometers rely on temperature differences to assess moisture content, while gravimetric hygrometers determine humidity by weighing the water absorbed by a hygroscopic substance. Each type serves distinct applications and industries, influencing their market demand and technological advancements.</p></p>
