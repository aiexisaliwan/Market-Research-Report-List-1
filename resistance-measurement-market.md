<p><p>The resistance measurement market can be categorized into three main types: low, medium, and high resistance measurement. Low resistance measurement focuses on evaluating materials with low resistive values, commonly found in electrical connections and grounding applications. Medium resistance measurement targets components like transformers and motors, where moderate resistance is relevant. High resistance measurement is utilized for insulation testing and leakage current assessments in high voltage systems, ensuring safety and performance in electrical installations. Each segment caters to specific industrial and operational needs.</p></p>
