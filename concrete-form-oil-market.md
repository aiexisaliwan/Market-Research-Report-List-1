<p><p>Concrete form oil is a release agent applied to formwork or molds before pouring concrete. It prevents the concrete from sticking to the molds, ensuring easy removal and a smooth finish. The product is widely used in construction, infrastructure, and precast concrete industries.</p><p>The Concrete Form Oil Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 4.2% during the forecast period.
