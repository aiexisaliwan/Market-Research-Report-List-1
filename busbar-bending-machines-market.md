Manual bending machines require operator skill to shape busbars, offering flexibility and lower initial costs. In contrast, CNC bending machines utilize computer numerical control for precision and automation, ensuring consistent and repeatable bends, which enhances efficiency in production. The choice between these types often depends on the production volume, required accuracy, and budget constraints of the manufacturing facility.</p></p>
