<p><p>The calcined petcoke market comprises several types, each serving distinct applications. Needle coke is characterized by its high purity and structure, making it ideal for producing electrodes in the steel and aluminum industries. Shot coke features a high density and is used primarily in the production of anodes. Sponge coke has a porous structure, suitable for fuel applications and coking processes. Honeycomb coke, with its specific morphology, finds applications in chemical and metallurgical processes, contributing to the diverse use of calcined petcoke.</p></p>
