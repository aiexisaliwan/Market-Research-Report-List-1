<p><strong>The Adhesive Dispensing Machine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cold Glue Dispensing Machine</li><li>Hot Melt Dispensing Machine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The adhesive dispensing machine market comprises two primary types: cold glue dispensing machines and hot melt dispensing machines. Cold glue machines utilize water-based or solvent-based adhesives, offering a versatile solution for various substrates, especially in packaging and assembly applications. In contrast, hot melt dispensing machines use thermoplastic adhesives, providing quick bonding and flexibility for high-speed production environments. Both types serve distinct industries, catering to specific adhesive needs based on temperature sensitivity, application methods, and desired bonding characteristics.</p></p>
