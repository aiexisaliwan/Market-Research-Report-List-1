<p><strong>The Indoor Heater Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Radiative Space Heater</li><li>Convective Space Heater</li><li>Filler Space Heater</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The indoor heater market comprises various types catering to different heating needs. Radiative space heaters emit infrared radiation to warm objects directly, making them efficient for localized heating. Convective space heaters distribute heat through air circulation, ideal for warming larger areas. Filler space heaters, which include oil-filled radiators, provide steady, long-lasting warmth by retaining heat. Other types encompass advanced technologies like ceramic and fan-assisted heaters, offering diverse options based on efficiency, space requirements, and user preferences.</p></p>
