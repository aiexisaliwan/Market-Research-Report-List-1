<p><p>Commercial sound masking systems can be categorized into two main types: networked and non-networked systems. Networked systems utilize a centralized management approach, allowing for remote monitoring and control, which enhances scalability and adaptability in larger spaces. They offer advanced features, such as integration with other building systems. In contrast, non-networked systems are standalone solutions that are simpler to install and operate, making them ideal for smaller environments where comprehensive management capabilities are less critical.
