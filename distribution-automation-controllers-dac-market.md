<p><p>Distribution Automation Controllers (DAC) are essential for enhancing electrical grid efficiency and reliability. The market is categorized into two types: distributed and centralized. </p><p>In distributed-type DAC systems, controllers are deployed across various locations within the network, allowing real-time data processing and localized decision-making, improving response times and resilience. Conversely, centralized DAC systems consolidate control functions in a single location, providing streamlined management and easier maintenance. Each type presents unique advantages based on operational requirements and grid complexity.</p></p>
