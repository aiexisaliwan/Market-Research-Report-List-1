<p><strong>The Wave Power Generation Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Direct Mechanical Transmission</li><li>Hydraulic Transmission</li><li>Pneumatic Actuator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Wave power generation equipment utilizes various transmission methods to convert ocean wave energy into usable electricity. Direct mechanical transmission involves the direct conversion of wave motion into mechanical energy, typically through oscillating devices. Hydraulic transmission uses the movement of waves to pressurize fluid, which drives hydraulic turbines to generate power. Pneumatic actuators harness wave energy by compressing air within a chamber, converting it into mechanical work that can drive generators. Each method offers distinct advantages in efficiency and design flexibility.</p></p>
