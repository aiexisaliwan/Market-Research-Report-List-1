<p><p>The Gelatin Service Tank market features three primary heating types: electric heating, steam heating, and oil heating. Electric heating utilizes electric coils or resistors to warm the gelatin, offering precise temperature control and quick heating. Steam heating uses steam generated from boilers, providing efficient and uniform heating but requires a steam supply system. Oil heating relies on heated oil circulated through coils, ensuring stable temperatures, though it may be less efficient and slower to respond compared to electric and steam options.</p></p>
