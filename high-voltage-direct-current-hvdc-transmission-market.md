<p><p>High Voltage Direct Current (HVDC) Transmission is a technology used to transmit electricity over long distances with minimal losses. This technology is particularly advantageous for connecting remote renewable energy sources like wind and solar to urban centers, facilitating the integration of clean energy.</p><p>The HVDC Transmission Market is experiencing significant growth, driven by increasing investments in renewable energy projects, the need for modernizing aging grid infrastructure, and the demand for efficient energy distribution systems.
